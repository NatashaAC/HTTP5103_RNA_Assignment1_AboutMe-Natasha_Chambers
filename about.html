Website Experience

I recently graduated from Humber College’s Web Design and Interactive Media program with an Advance Diploma and I have a background in Engineering.

I’ve always had a fascination with technology and constantly wondered how devices worked. In high school I took auto shop and construction classes. I had fun rebuilding mowers, engines, and fixing tires. Overall, I just enjoyed working with my hands more than doing research or writing. The Electronics Engineering Technician program at Humber was a way to get the best of both worlds, but then my interests changed. I started to wonder how programs, websites, apps and games worked. I wanted to learn how to create my own websites, apps, and games, which lead me to enter the Web Design and Interactive Media program.
